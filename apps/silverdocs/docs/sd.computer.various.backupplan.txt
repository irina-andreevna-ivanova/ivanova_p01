OS-reinstall backup plan
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   1. My Documents - "c:\Documents and Settings\user.name\My Documents"
   2. Desktop-ul - "c:\Documents and Settings\user.name\Desktop"
   3. Quick Launch - "c:\Documents and Settings\user.name\Application Data\Microsoft\Internet Explorer\Quick Launch"
   4. Favorites IE : "c:\Documents and Settings\user.name\Favorites"
   5. E-Mailuri: Office Outlook – fisierele .pst de obicei din calea "C:\Documents and Settings\user.name\Local Settings\Application Data\Microsoft\Outlook\..". Fisierele .ost nu trebuie salvate continutul lor fiind pastrat pe server
        Outlook Express – Google: “Outlook Express HowTo Save&Restore e-Mails & Settings”
   6. E-Mail Rules: Office Outlook – Tools-> Rules Wizard->Options…
        Outlook Express – Google: “Outlook Express HowTo Save&Restore e-Mails & Settings”
   7. E-Mail Address Book: Office Outlook – e inclus in fisierul .pst dar daca sunt probleme -> Google…
        Outlook Express - Google: “Outlook Express HowTo Save&Restore e-Mails & Settings”
   8. E-Mail Addresses Autocomplete – pt Office Outlook - salveaza fisierul "c:\Documents and Settings\useru.tau\Application Data\Microsoft\Outlook\Outlook.NK2"
   9. E-Mail Signature: "c:\Documents and Settings\user.nameApplication Data\Microsoft\Signatures"
  10. Arhiva E-Mailuri: Office Outlook – fisierele .pst de obicei din calea "C:\Documents and Settings\user.name\Local Settings\Application Data\Microsoft\Outlook\..". Fisierele .ost nu trebuie salvate continutul lor fiind pastrat pe server
  11. Office 2003 – All settings: Start->Programs->MS Office->MS Office Tools->MS Office 2003 Save my settings wizard (include: look-ul interfetei, mail account settings, junk email settings, rules, etc); pt refacere tot acesta se foloseste dar cu Restore
  12. Yahoo Messenger Archive – Win XP: "c:\Program Files\Yahoo!\Messenger\Profiles\user.name"
      - Win Vista/Win7: „C:\Users\user.name\AppData\Local\VirtualStore\Program Files\Yahoo!\Messenger\Profiles”
  13. Skype User List – daca instalezi ultima versiune atunci ar trebui sa fie salvate pe serverele Skype, alfel copiezi continutul:C:\Documents and Settings\userul.tau\Application Data\Skype\skype_ID_name. Pentru a salva chat-history – copiati continutul foledrului c:\Documents and Settings\user.name\Application Data\Skype\[skypeusername]  in directorul echivalent de pe masina tinta
  14. Sa notezi ce aveai share-uit.
  15. Sa notezi ce drive-uri aveai map-uit
  16. History la IE - "c:\Documents and Settings\user.name\Local Settings\History\History.IE5"
  17. Firefox “Bookmarks”:   Bookmarks -> Organize Bookmarks  si in Bookmark Manager: File->Export . Ca sa salvati si colectia de username/password – copiati folderol c:\documents and sttings\username\Application Data\Mozilla Firefox\Pofiles\[nume indescifrabil+ nume profil]\ in acelasi director de pe calculatorul unde vreti sa transfrati (asta daca ati instalat déjà Mozilla)
  18. Total Commander - lista FTP-urilor fisier C:\Windows\wcx_ftp.ini [Cristian Caprar] sau in c:\documents and settings\user.name\
  19. Total Commander - setari - C:\Windows\wincmd.ini [Cristian Caprar] sau in c:\documents and settings\user.name\
  20. Stuff personal si proiectele de ex: C:\Documents and Settings\user.name\IdeaProjects sau C:\Work
  21. Setari IntellijIdea – directorul "C:\Documents and Settings\user.name\.IntelliJIdea\config"
        - fisierul "C:\Documents and Settings\user.name\.IntelliJIdea\system\idea.license"
        - directorul "C:\Documents and Settings\user.name\.IntelliJIdea\system\plugins"
  22. Backup DB-ul de Oracle si cel de SQLServer – How? Ask DB Admins
  23. Winamp : Skin – "C:\Program Files\Winamp\Skins"
               Plugins: "C:\Program Files\Winamp\Plugins"
  24. Cerfificate folosite : export, save : Run mmc.exe -> File ->Add/Remove Snap-in -> Select Certificates -> Add – My User account -> Finnish -> Browse for Certificates-Current User-> Personal -> Certificates – Right Click on Certificate ->All tasks -> Export -> Yes, export the private key-> Select Personal Information Exchange (PFX) -> Ckeck Include all certificates path and Export all extended properties - > Type/confirm a password -> select file location and file name-> Finish
  25. PGP Keys
  26. Fisierul C:\WINDOWS\system32\drivers\etc\hosts
  27. iTunes: backups de telefon facut de iTunes (daca aveti iPhone), precum si colectia de aplicatii pe care le-ati downloadat cu iTunes si sincronizat cu telefonul – le gasiti in My Documents\My Music\iTunes
  28. Salvati notebook folders folosite de MS One Note (de obicei in My Documents\My Notebook)
  29. Salvati Firefox Profiles (daca aveti ceva custom GreaseMonkey scripts pe care vreti sa le pastrati...) – din app data\Firefox
  30. Sincronizeaza Offline Files and Folders cu sursa master (daca aveti asa ceva), incat sa nu pierdeti modificarile locale facute offline
  31. Daca folositi Pidgin si nu YM, salvati profilul si logurile (se gasesc in c:\documents and settings\user.name\Application Data\.purple)
  32. Putty: HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\SimonTatham
